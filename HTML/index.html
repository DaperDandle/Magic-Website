Welcome To Command Club KC!

We're a group of Magic players who love the commander format. We understand that the point of Magic is to have fun, it's not all about winning! We play casual games but theres always room for our more competitive friends too. We host a monthly commander tournament with prizes for top finishers. Come join our community, we hope to see you at our next event!
